# Power BI Dashboard

## Overview
This folder contains the **Power BI dashboard** developed for **Task 3 – Marketing Campaign & Conversion Performance Analysis** as part of the **Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship**.

The dashboard analyzes the performance of a bank marketing campaign by evaluating customer conversion rates, campaign effectiveness, customer demographics, and previous campaign outcomes. It provides an interactive environment for exploring customer behavior and identifying key factors influencing successful subscriptions.

## Dashboard File

### Task_03.pbix
This Power BI dashboard includes interactive reports, dynamic filtering, and business-focused visualizations to evaluate the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

- Customers Contacted
- Successful Subscriptions
- Conversion Rate
- Average Customer Balance
- Average Campaign Contacts

### 📈 Visualizations

- Subscription Distribution (Donut Chart)
- Subscriptions by Job Category (Treemap)
- Monthly Subscription Trend (Line Chart)
- Conversion by Contact Method (100% Stacked Bar Chart)
- Subscriptions by Previous Campaign Outcome (Bar Chart)
- Subscriptions by Age Group (Clustered Column Chart)

### 🎛 Interactive Filters

The dashboard allows dynamic filtering using:
- Month
- Job
- Education

All KPI cards and visualizations update automatically based on the selected filters.

## Business Insights
The dashboard helps identify:

- Overall marketing campaign conversion performance.
- Customer segments with the highest subscription rates.
- The most effective contact methods.
- Monthly campaign performance trends.
- Impact of previous campaign outcomes on customer conversion.
- Age groups and professions contributing to successful subscriptions.
